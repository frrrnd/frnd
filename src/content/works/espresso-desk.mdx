Espresso Desk is an online guide to discovering the best cafés for working. Designed as a collaborative platform, it allows users to share and explore inspiring, work-friendly spots around the city—turning the search for the perfect remote workspace into a community-driven experience.


## Role 

I led the UX/UI design of the platform, creating an interface that encourages user contribution while keeping the experience clean and intuitive. I focused on designing smooth navigation, clear content structure, and a visual identity that reflects the brand’s warm and creative atmosphere.
