    **Trackr** is a concept app for WatchOS designed to monitor running sports. It shows your real-time location on the map while you walk, run, or engage in other speed exercises. At the end of each session, it displays detailed graphs with session duration, distance covered, calories burned, average heart rate, and a comparison with previous days.
